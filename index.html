Vehicle GPRS Gateway

Description:

The GPRS Gateway is a receiving terminal with GPS positioning function and a gateway for temperature and humidity sensors.
This product is usually used on logistics vehicles and other modes of transport or can be placed in warehouses and other fixed places.
It can receive a plurality of temperature and/or humidity sensors to collect temperature and humidity data and uploard to the server through GPRS.
Easy to install, its high stability and fast data transmission makes it the ideal real-time monitoring system for cold chain logistics or medical transports.

Features:
  • Maximum 300m distance communicating
  • 4500mah battery can work for 1 day without external power supply
  • 100s of sensors can be recognized at a time
  • Alarm notification
  • Status light indication
